ABOUT DOMAIN

Over the past ten years, data has transformed how we see the world. The approximately 2.5 quintillion bytes of data generated daily worldwide includes all of the emails, texts, and YouTube movies we send and receive. The ability of businesses large and small to extract valuable insights from the vast amounts of data they deal with is crucial. An analyzer of data does just that. They analyze statistical data, and transform it into informative data that businesses and organizations may utilize to make important decisions.

More and more businesses across all industries rely on data to help them make crucial business decisions, such as which products to develop, which markets to pursue, what investments to make, or which customers to target. Additionally, they are leveraging data to pinpoint weak points in the company that require attention.

As a result, data analysts are sought after by the largest corporations in the world, making data analysis one of the most in-demand careers globally. Data analyst benefits and pay merely reflect the high demand for this position, which is expected to continue expanding rapidly
