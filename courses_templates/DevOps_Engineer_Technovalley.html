About Domain

The DevOps curriculum gives a professional capacity to manage and track different versions of source code, automate testing procedures, manage to cluster, and perform continuous monitoring with the help of many tools.
DevOps provides you with in-depth knowledge of various DevOps tools, including Git, Jenkins, Docker, Ansible, Terraform, Kubernetes, Prometheus, and Grafana. After the successful completion of DevOps course you become a certified practitioner through best practices in Continuous Development, Configuration Management, Continuous Integration, and finally, Continuous Monitoring of software throughout its development life cycle.

Who Can Apply for the Course?

Beginners: If you're new to programming or have limited experience in DevOps , this course is ideal for you. You'll learn DevOps tools and gradually progress to advanced topics with practical training.

IT Professionals: If you're working in the IT industry and want to enhance your DevOps skills, this course can help you acquire the latest techniques and trends in the field.

Students: Whether you're pursuing a degree in computer science or want to add a new skill to your academic portfolio, Technovalley's DevOps course can provide you with the practical skills and knowledge you need to excel in your career.

Diploma Holders: If you hold a diploma in computer science or a related field and want to become a proficient DevOps Engineer, this course can help you master core and advanced concepts of DevOps with industry experts and practical training.
